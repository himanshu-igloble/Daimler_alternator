## What this service does (and its honest limits)
- **Which trucks** (validated, AUROC 0.927): ranks failure risk; 1/15 in-service trucks are HIGH_RISK.
- **When (fleet-level)**: replacement window = empirical **601 d** (IQR 578–652; ≈120440 km / 4538 engine-hr, estimated). Survival median 718.5 d is shown elsewhere as long-biased context only.
- **Early warning**: GED2 storm alarm — verified ~21-day lead on **1** historical truck (VIN1); **rests on n=1**, re-verified on every new failure. Most failures give no precursor.
- **Per-truck RUL date**: deliberately NOT emitted — it loses to the fleet clock (142 d vs 50 d backtest).
