## 7. Limitations

1. **n = 10 failure events.** Every survival quantity has wide uncertainty; the fleet Weibull is unstable enough that an empirical median is a better point estimate.
2. **No per-truck timing signal.** VSI is flat to failure for 8/10 trucks; only GED=2 (2/10) gives a real precursor. A precise per-truck failure date is not extractable from the 6 CAN channels.
3. **km / engine-hours are estimated** (speed-integrated, not odometer/hour-meter).
4. **Classifier frozen.** No retraining; ridge_prob is a static whole-life score (its risk discrimination is borrowed by the RUL layer for ranking, not timing).
